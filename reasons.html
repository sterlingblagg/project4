  • Reasons for Rebellion

We are rebelling for our freedom in our speech with like-minded individuals in terms of online social interaction. Every person online, no matter how similar we may seem, and how like-minded our goals are, is very different in the scope of personality. We have the right to be able to talk about whatever we want with our friends, even if we have never met them in real life! Let's rejoice in our ability to communicate, and hope that our rebellions will cause us to gain our independent speech online!
